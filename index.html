About Pinnacle Oil & Gas

Founded with a vision to power progress, Pinnacle Oil & Gas has grown into a trusted name in the energy industry. From our humble beginnings as a regional supplier, we have steadily expanded our reach, building a reputation for quality, reliability, and excellence in every product we deliver.
Over the years, we have achieved significant milestones that reflect our commitment to growth and service. From scaling our distribution network across multiple regions to meeting the energy demands of major industries, Pinnacle Oil & Gas has consistently risen to the challenge.
Today, we stand proud as a leading provider of diesel, petroleum, and gas solutions — driven by the same values that have guided us from the start: integrity, quality, and a relentless dedication to our customers.
